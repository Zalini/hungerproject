Hunger is a daily issue for an alarming amount of New York City residents.

People, from the youth to the elderly, are plagued with reality that their next meal is not guaranteed.

This is an issue that we can combat, but we need your help.


Facts

  • ➤ The unfortunate reality is too many people go without the proper nutrition they need each day to properly function in New York City

  • ➤ Food insecure households usually have to decide between purchasing nutritious foods and paying housing or medical bills

  • ➤ The average cost of a meal in New York City is $3.37

  • ➤ A lack of nutritious foods impacts both physical and mental well-being

We operate 7 days a week, working each day with the community to provide food to 100 families and counting. We also provide scheduled drop-offs for those who are not able to come to our location.
