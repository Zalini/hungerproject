Food is not an option, it is a necessity

What we do

Operating 7 days a week, Operation FEED NYC works tirelessly to bring food items to our fellow New Yorkers in need. It starts with the procurement of resources; monetary donations, volunteers and food donations. With these resources, we are able to provide food to over 100 families a week with scheduled drop offs for those who are not able to come to our location.

For more information on how to get food assistance please call the Hunger Hotline
